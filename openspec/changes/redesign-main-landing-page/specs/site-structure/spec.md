## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Homepage Content
The homepage at solidframe.ai SHALL serve as the primary brand introduction and lead generation entry point.

#### Scenario: Visitor lands on homepage
- **WHEN** a visitor navigates to solidframe.ai
- **THEN** they see a clear positioning statement identifying SolidFrame as a specialist for trades businesses (HVAC, plumbers, roofers, electricians)
- **AND** the page communicates that SolidFrame makes businesses better, faster, and saves them money and time
- **AND** the primary call-to-action is booking a free 20-minute discovery call

#### Scenario: Discovery call offer messaging
- **WHEN** a visitor reads the discovery call section
- **THEN** they understand that the call will:
  - Identify their main business bottlenecks
  - Demonstrate relevant AI tools or solutions
  - Show them the possibilities of what AI can do for their business
- **AND** the messaging conveys confidence ("we know once you see what's possible, you'll want to work with us")

#### Scenario: Target audience clarity
- **WHEN** a visitor evaluates if SolidFrame is for them
- **THEN** they clearly see that SolidFrame exclusively serves:
  - HVAC companies
  - Plumbing companies
  - Roofing companies
  - Electrical contractors
- **AND** the specialization is positioned as a strength ("this is all we do")

#### Scenario: Simplified page structure
- **WHEN** a visitor scrolls through the homepage
- **THEN** they encounter a focused experience with:
  - Hero section with positioning statement
  - Brief explanation of who we serve and value delivered
  - Discovery call booking CTA
- **AND** the page does NOT include pricing tiers, detailed feature lists, or FAQ sections
