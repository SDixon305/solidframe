- Low Volume (≤25 calls/week; ≤100/month):

  Call Center/Triage:    $350/month (emergency triage/booking)

- High Volume (26–40 calls/week; 101–170/month):

  Traditional Answering: $410/month (messages only)
  Call Center/Triage:    $1,100/month (emergency triage/booking)

Cost Comparison Summary:
• Agency pricing sits between basic answering service and full-featured call center.
• Demonstrates significant potential savings vs. advanced triage/booking alternatives.
• Tiered model matches industry standards (higher volume = greater cost).
• All estimates are higher-side but well within reasonable industry expectations.
