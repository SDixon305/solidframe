# Copywriting Assets (Derived from Offer Definition)

## Landing Page Copy

### Hero Section
**Headline:** Never Miss an After-Hours Emergency Call Again.
**Subheadline:** The AI Receptionist that answers 24/7, filters tire-kickers, and dispatches your techs only when it pays.
**CTA Button:** Listen to a Live Demo

### The Problem (Agitation)
"It's 2 AM. Your phone rings. Is it a $5,000 system replacement? Or someone asking if you change filters?"
- You can't answer every call yourself (burnout).
- Human answering services are slow, sound robotic, and can't troubleshoot.
- Every missed call is a customer calling your competitor.

### The Solution (The AI Advantage)
Meet your new **AI Service Manager**.
- **Instant Response:** Answers in 0.5 seconds.
- **Smart Triage:** Knows the difference between "No Heat" (Emergency) and "Schedule Maintenance" (Routine).
- **CRM Integration:** Logs every call, records the audio, and syncs with your schedule.
- **Escalation:** Only wakes you up for the jobs that matter.

### Social Proof / Trust
"Trusted by forward-thinking HVAC owners to handle their overflow and after-hours calls."

### FAQ
**Q: Does it sound like a robot?**
A: No. It uses advanced voice AI (Vapi) to sound natural, pause, and interrupt just like a human.

**Q: What if it messes up?**
A: You have full access to call recordings. Plus, we can set it to "fail safe" by forwarding to a human if it gets confused.

## Outreach Script (Cold Email/DM) Snippet
"Hey [Name], I built an AI that handles after-hours HVAC calls so you don't have to. It triages emergencies vs. routine stuff and books the appt. Want to hear a recording of it handling a 'No Heat' call?"
